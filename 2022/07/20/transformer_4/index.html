Stanford Seminar - Transformers United Other ingredients of transformer

Author: Jingyi WAN Date: July 20, 2022  15:35:51

Positional Representations

  • Inspired by RNN works: sequential ordering
  • Self-attention is an unordered function of its inputs, because every word is attending to every other word.

Nonlinearities

  • In self-attention, it’s all linear because it’s all matrix multiplication
  • With deep learning model, nonlinearities happens

Masking

  • allows to parallelize the operations while not looking at the future
  • keeps information about the future from leaking to past
  • used in the decoder
